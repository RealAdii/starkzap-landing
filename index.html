FAQ

Who can build on Starkzap?

Anyone with access to the internet can now ship products in minutes.

Do my users need to know anything about web3?

Not at all. Starkzap abstracts away wallets, gas fees, and blockchain complexity. Your users get a familiar web2 experience while you get the benefits of onchain infrastructure.

Can I bridge tokens from other chains?

Absolutely. Starkzap makes cross-chain bridging effortless. Move tokens from Ethereum, Solana, and other chains onto Starknet with just a few lines of code.

How easy are swaps?

Insanely easy. One function call and you're done. Starkzap routes through AVNU and Ekubo to guarantee the best price every time. No manual DEX hunting required.
